# Operations Content Plan

## Purpose

Turn product or business goals into executable operations content and channel plans.

## Triggers

- A feature or campaign needs operational rollout.
- A channel content calendar or SOP is required.
- User feedback needs an operations response plan.

## Inputs

- Goal, audience, channel, constraints, and assets.
- Product message and approval boundaries.
- Existing content or SOP references.

## Workflow

1. Define audience, channel, message, and conversion action.
2. Create content structure and publishing steps.
3. Identify assets, approval needs, and risks.
4. Define metrics and feedback collection.
5. Hand off to operations execution or product feedback.

## Outputs

- Content plan.
- Publishing checklist.
- SOP or campaign task list.
- Metrics plan.

## Quality Gate

- Audience and action are clear.
- Content does not make unapproved commitments.
- Metrics connect to the campaign goal.

## Failure Routes

- Missing product positioning: return to Product Manager Agent.
- Missing approval: route to Project Manager Agent.
- Risky public claim: require human review.
