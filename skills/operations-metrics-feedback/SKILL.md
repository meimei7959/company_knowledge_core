# Operations Metrics Feedback

## Purpose

Convert operations results into decisions, product feedback, or reusable knowledge.

## Triggers

- Campaign or channel data is available.
- Community feedback reveals product issues.
- Operations work has completed and needs review.

## Inputs

- Metrics, comments, feedback, and channel context.
- Campaign goal and timeline.
- Product or project linkage.

## Workflow

1. Summarize what happened and what changed.
2. Separate signal from anecdote.
3. Identify product, knowledge, or operations follow-up.
4. Create tasks or knowledge material with evidence.
5. Report risks and recommended next action.

## Outputs

- Operations feedback report.
- Product or knowledge follow-up task.
- Metrics and evidence refs.

## Quality Gate

- Conclusions are tied to data or examples.
- Follow-up has owner and scope.
- Reusable lessons include source evidence.

## Failure Routes

- Data too weak: mark confidence low and request more data.
- Product impact: route to Product Manager Agent.
- Reusable lesson: route to Knowledge Engineering Agent.
